flavours

Using only the finest ingredients, Jane is passionate about creating distinctive and delectable flavour combinations, each of which can be tailored to suit you and your wedding day. Featuring alternating layers of sponge, buttercream, fruit-infused preserves and thick sauces, Jane has expertly curated a menu that will ensure your wedding cake is the nothing short of exceptional.
